# Informed Consent — Cognitive Copilot User Study

**Study title.** Evaluation of Cognitive Copilot, a local-first adaptive tutor with grounded retrieval.
**Principal investigators.** Khadimul Islam Mahi (2107076), Sumaiya Akter (2107080).
**Supervisor.** Prof. Md. Nazirul Hasan Shawon, KUET CSE.
**Contact.** mahi.jess9t9@gmail.com.

## Purpose

You are invited to participate in a two-week evaluation of an AI tutoring system built as part of our CSE 3200 capstone. We want to measure whether the system meaningfully improves topic-level quiz accuracy and whether it feels trustworthy and usable.

## What you will do

1. Attend a 30-minute onboarding session.
2. Take a 10-question pre-test covering two topics.
3. Use the tutor freely for one week on your own schedule.
4. Take a parallel 10-question post-test.
5. Fill out a 10-item usability questionnaire + one recommendation score.
6. Optionally, participate in a 15-minute semi-structured interview (audio-recorded; transcription is automated and anonymised before anyone reads it).

Total time commitment: approximately **2 hours** over two weeks.

## What we collect

If you consent, we collect:

- Your responses to the pre-test, post-test, and usability questionnaire.
- System telemetry: quiz attempts, topic mastery (Beta-posterior α and β), LLM call metadata (route, latency, token counts, status — **not the text of your prompts or the tutor's replies**).
- Anonymised interview transcripts, if you participate.

We **do not** collect:

- Your name, student ID, email, or any other identifier in the analysis dataset.
- The content of your conversations with the tutor.
- Screenshots, screen recordings, or keystrokes.

## Risks

- **Minimal.** The system runs locally on your device or on a private server; it does not make network calls to commercial LLM APIs.
- A remote possibility of discomfort from quiz feedback. You may stop at any time.
- The study data is kept on a Postgres instance behind a password; backups are encrypted at rest.

## Benefits

- Free access to the tutor during the study.
- Tutor-generated study packets covering your weakest topics, delivered after the post-test.
- Credit in the acknowledgements of the final project report (optional, revocable).

## Your rights

- Participation is **voluntary**. Declining has no effect on your course grade or relationship with the instructor.
- You may **withdraw at any time**. On withdrawal, your data is deleted within 7 days.
- You may request **a copy of your data** at any time via the contact email above.
- Published material will report only aggregate statistics or fully anonymised quotes. If we wish to quote you identifiably, we will ask for a separate written sign-off.

## Data retention

Study data is deleted 90 days after the award submission unless you separately grant extended retention for research replication.
